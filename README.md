Kenya Food Price Analysis (2006–2024)

**Project Overview**

This project analyzes food price trends in Kenya between 2006 and 2024 using Excel, SQL, and Power BI.

**Business Questions**

* Have food prices generally increased over time?
* Which commodities experienced the highest price growth?
* How do food prices vary across regions?
* What differences exist between wholesale and retail prices?

**Data Cleaning**

Key cleaning steps included:
* Converting text dates to the proper date format
* Extracting years for trend analysis
* Standardizing units (kg, g, ml, liters)
* Creating a standardized price-per-unit metric
* Removing rows(latitude and longitude)

**Tools Used**

* Excel
* MySQL
* Power BI

**Key Findings**

* Food prices generally increased over time.
* Some commodities experienced significantly higher growth than others.
* Regional price differences were evident across the study period.
